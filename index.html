The Background of the Nissan Skyline r34
The R34 Skyline GT-R was introduced in 1998. The car's technologically advanced display unit set the model apart from the generation before them. The R34 Skyline has a RB26DETT twin-turbo I6, ATTESA E-TS All-wheel drive system and the Super-HICAS four-wheel steering. The car was nickname the GT-R "Godzilla". Production of the Skyline GT-R ended in August 2002.
